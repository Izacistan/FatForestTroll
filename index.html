Services

Design

I design intuitive websites that are easy to navigate and enjoyable to use. All of my websites are designed with simplicity in mind. A simple layout makes your website more enjoyable to use and allows users to get the information they want faster. Every person and every business is different, and I will work with you to design the best website to suit your needs.

Compatability

From your smartphone to your tablet, and from your laptop to your desktop, websites should work across all devices and screen sizes. All of my websites will work and function properly across all modern browsers and all screen sizes, so you do not need to worry about clients missing important information or having a bad experience on your website.

Code

I start with an HTML/CSS foundation, and then build upon that with JavaScript and/or PHP to create any corresponding website features. While I love custom building websites, I understand that sometimes WordPress sites are a better fit for some clients. I will work with you to determine best route for your website.
